Flower Knowledge

Rose

Roses are popular flowers that come in a variety of colors. They are often associated with love and romance.

Rose petals are edible and can be used in culinary preparations or for decorative purposes.

Lily

Lilies are elegant flowers known for their large, showy blooms and pleasant fragrance.

They come in various colors, including white, pink, yellow, and orange.

Tulip

Tulips are spring-blooming flowers that are known for their vibrant colors and distinctive cup-shaped blooms.

They are commonly associated with the Netherlands and are a symbol of spring.
